our mission

At Chidot School of Technology, our mission is to empower the next generation of innovative technologists and creators. We are committed to providing a dynamic and inclusive learning environment where students cultivate their passion for technology, harness their creativity, and develop the skills required to thrive in the rapidly evolving digital world. Through rigorous education, hands-on experiences, and a strong emphasis on ethical practices, we aim to equip our students with the knowledge and expertise needed to lead and contribute to the advancement of technology for the betterment of society.

our vision

Our vision at Chidot School of Technology is to be a global leader in technology education, recognized for producing exceptional graduates who shape the future of technology innovation. We envision a community of learners united by their curiosity, dedication, and commitment to excellence. By fostering an atmosphere of collaboration, critical thinking, and lifelong learning, we aspire to empower our students to become visionary leaders, problem solvers, and ethical technologists who drive positive change on a local and global scale. Through cutting-edge programs, pioneering research, and a focus on human-centric solutions, we strive to be a catalyst for innovation that transforms industries and enriches lives.
